Massively

Prototyping - Interaction Design - Visual Design

The Problem

Massively is an interactive text messaging application where users can engage with fictional characters. I was hired by Team 2 and House of Cool to handle the design of the application and comply with the brand guidelines.

Team & Role

My role for this engagement was to work with Team 2 (development team) to design and build a product that would be easy and fun for users to interact with.

The Massively UI was designed with in reference to the wireframes that Team 2 and I had finalized. We designed the application to resemble an easy test messaging application. The goal was to get to market as quick as possible.

The profile is an area where users can easily get an overview of the stories they have, the ability to purchase coins to buy exclusive stories, and where the user can see their achievements.

The description of a story has all the information a user needs to understand what a story is about. Once the user activates a story they will receive a message from the lead character.

The Massively application allows users to interact with characters from different fictional worlds. You can easily go through the application to message characters, get stories, earn achievements for rewards, and share with friends.
